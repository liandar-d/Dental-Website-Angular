Teeth Whitening

We offer professional whitening as it is a fast, effective and safe way to whiten your teeth. The options available include the in-house treatment which immediately whitens, or the customized trays which allow you to complete the treatment gradually at home.

In-chair treatment can provide immediate results and because a dental professional is involved, the process is safe, reliable and hassle free.

The take away kit will include the bleaching guards and the bleaching agent.It will take a few applications to get the desired result.

After the treatment is completed, we recommend to stay clear of some colour rich foods like beetroot and curry, and drinks like tea and red wine as these can discolour the teeth. Other lifestyle choices like smoking may also encourage the teeth to re-stain. However, these can be removed through a Hygienist Scale and Polish and bleaching trays can be used to help top up and maintain whiteness.
