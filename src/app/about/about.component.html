About Us

Our mission is to develop long-term relationships with our patients and to ensure distinguished service through personalized, honest, ethical and informed care. We aim to help our community achieve excellent oral healthcare through high quality, multidisciplinary and cost efficient dental services and procedures.

  • Over 25 years of community practice
  • Accurately assess your dental condition
  • Educate you about the problem and procedure
  • Provide safety, comfort and security in a warm and welcoming environment
  • Help you afford the treatment you want and deserve
  • Provide supportive hygiene care following treatment to help you maintain optimum results
