Luisa Antonio

Hygienist

Luisa Antonio is a 2009 BSc Oral Health graduate with a dual scope of practice in dental therapy and dental hygiene. As a new graduate Luisa worked 3 days a week for the Waitemata DHB and 2 days in a private practice to accomodate both her scopes of clinical work for 8 years. Luisa recently has in the past 4 years committed her work career to focusing on patients in private practises.


She lives in Greenhithe North Shore, but has chosen to travel to Ruakaka for work on a Wednesday and Thursday. With over 10years of experience, Luisa believes in promoting basic oral hygiene care, providing educational information for patients with medically compromised conditions and early prevention and intervention in dentistry. She is passionate about providing treatment in the best and most comfortable way for her patients.
