Deborah Dixon

Practice Manager

Deborah has been with this practice for 20 years. She strives to make sure that everyone is treated to a warm welcoming relaxed atmosphere that makes even the nerviest patient feel at ease – her sense of humour helps too. “No one likes going to the dentist, so we try to make everyone feel relaxed and assure them that they are in good hands” Deborah is not only just the Practice Manager, she is called upon to assistant the dentist if one of our staff team is not available.

In her spare time Deborah is an Equestrian Rider with over 40 years’ experience. She has competed successful at top level equestrian events which includes Horse of the Year. She is also a qualified Royal Agricultural Society Equestrian Judge.
