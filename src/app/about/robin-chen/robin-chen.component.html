Dr Robin Chen

Principal Dentist

Robin graduated with a Bachelor of Dental Surgery from India in 1989 . Following practicing there for a few years he decided to move to New Zealand where he passed his registration exams (DCNZ ) in 1996 . He went into private practice in Auckland where he worked for 2 years before making the move to Invercargill in 1998 . He has enjoyed living and serving the community In Southland for over 20 years . He then decided to move up north to Ruakaka to be close to his parents who live in Auckland.


Robin is honest , friendly and very competent in all aspects of general dentistry . He is a dedicated clinician and enjoys different people and cultures.
