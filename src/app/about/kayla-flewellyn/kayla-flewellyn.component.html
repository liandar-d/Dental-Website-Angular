Kayla Flewellyn

Dental Assistant

I only started working as a dental assistant at Gentle Dental since May. I was always interested in this line of work and when the position was offered to me I jumped at it. I have learnt a lot while working alongside of Robin.


What I love about working at this clinic is that the patients that come in feeling nervous and stressed, gain the confidence and trust in us which takes away their fear. The other thing I enjoy is that no day is the same every day is switched up whether it's meeting new people or learning something completely different.


My hobbies include visiting new places with my partner walking our dog's four-wheel driving and surfing.
